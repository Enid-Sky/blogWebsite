Favorite Coffee Shops

Tuesday, March 24, 2020

There are so many great coffee shops in San Francisco, that it was hard to narrow down my favorite five. Below are my top recommendations. Click on them to visit their website and check out their menus.

  • Andytown Coffee Roasters: 3655 Lawton St.
  • Henry's House of Coffee: 1618 Noriega St.
  • Ballast Coffee: 329 W Portal Ave
  • Snowbird Coffee: 1352A 9th Ave
  • Flywheel Coffee: 672 Stanyan St.

Amy is a writter and coffee lover. She lives in San Francisco. with her husband and their dog, Mocha. Photo by Ramiz Dedaković.
