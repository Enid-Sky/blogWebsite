Coffee and Books

Tuesday, March 1, 2020

There's nothing better than afternoon coffee and a good book. My new year's resolution this year was to read two books every month for a year. I'm not very good at sticking to resolutions but a friend of mine told me that she is better at accomplishing small tasks when paired with something she loves. I have no doubt that pairing a book with my favorite coffee will encourage me to keep my goal of reading everyday. Here are five coffee related books for you!

  1. The various flavors of coffee
  2. The Espressologist
  3. Mugs of Love
  4. The Coffee Roaster's Companion
  5. Through the Grinder

Amy is a writter and coffee lover. She lives in San Francisco. with her husband and their dog, Mocha. Photo by Ramiz Dedaković.
